## Top 3 to implement first (chosen for: amplifies the already-running
cold machine, fully automatable, zero budget)

1. **#1 Audit-completion email capture + PDF nurture** — turns the
   audit page from a one-shot demo into an owned-audience asset. Every
   cold click gets 5 more automated chances to convert. (Build prompt in
   09; respects the "no signup before result" promise by asking AFTER
   the result.)
2. **#2 + #16 Prefilled audit links + forward P.S.** — near-zero build,
   lifts completion rate of the traffic we're already paying the domain
   reputation for, and adds a free referral surface with its own c-code.
3. **#3 PLAYBOOK reply-keyword funnel** — already written into Email 3;
   the poller auto-fulfills. Buys deliverability (replies!) and delivers
   real value that makes the audit CTA feel earned.

Everything else is sequenced: #8/#14 feed copy this week (inputs, not
channels) · #6 rides the monthly ROC refresh · #11 is calendared for
September · #4/#7/#13 start after first paid user · #12/#17 are month-3
options.
